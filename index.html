About me

Hello! I'm Harika Sasapu, a passionate technologist and Cornell University graduate (Class of 2024) with a Master's in Engineering Management, specializing in Product Management. With a solid foundation in software engineering and a keen interest in bridging technology and business, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking to every project I take on.

My journey has been marked by diverse experiences, from building scalable web architectures as a Software Engineer at FactSet and American Express to driving product roadmaps and user-centric designs during my internships at Cognizant, Siemens, and Pillow. Industry projects with global leaders like Amazon, Chubb, and MakeMyTrip have honed my skills in analytics, machine learning, and customer-focused product solutions, while hands-on work in healthcare and supply chain analytics using Power BI, Tableau, and SQL has deepened my appreciation for data-driven decision-making.

Outside the professional sphere, I wear many hats—Zumba Certified Trainer, Scuba PADI Open Water Diver, and Artistic Roller Skating State Champion. I also love diving into books like "Inspired: How to Create Products Customers Love"and "Clean Code," which continually refine my perspectives on products and engineering.

I’m a firm believer in the power of curiosity and adaptability. Whether it’s architecting AI-driven systems, designing seamless user journeys, or analyzing complex datasets, I thrive on creating impact through thoughtful solutions. As I look toward the future, I’m excited about opportunities in Product Management, Development, and Analytics where I can leverage my skills to build innovative and meaningful solutions. Let’s connect and create something extraordinary!
